\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The class {\ttfamily \doxyref{Material\+Quantity}{p.}{classsrc_1_1_material_quantity}} represents the quantity of a given material, together with the date of its addition to the system. 

This class allows you to associate a material with an amount and record the date of the transaction.
